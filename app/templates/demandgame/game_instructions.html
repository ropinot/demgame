Game instructions

Dashboard components

The planning information in the dashboard are (per each period):

  • Received: quantity ordered in the past and received at the beginning of the period (depends on regular and spot leadtimes).
  • Stock: quantity on hand at the beginning of the period, including the quantity received in the period (see RECEIVED)
  • Forecast: forecast over some periods
  • Demand: actual demand
  • Order: quantity ordered from the regular supplier
  • Spot: quantity ordered from tbe spot supplier
  • Sales: actual sales
  • Lost sales: unsatisfied demand
  • Error: forecasting error

Game parameters

The game uses the following parameters (see also the Game data ):

  • Selling price: price per unit to the final customer.
  • Regular/spot supplier lead time: number of periods from the order to the goods receipt (i.e. 1 periods = order today and receive tomorrow).
  • Regular/spot purchasing cost: price per unit paid to the suppliers.
  • Stock holding cost: unit cost per period for on-hand material
  • Lost sales cost: cost per unit of lost sales, computed as the difference between the demand in the period and the quantity on-hand at the beginning of the period.
  • Regular/spot order cost: cost for each order
  • Minimum purchase size: minimum size of the order accepted by the supplier.
  • Spot supplier yield: percentage of the ordered quantity delivered on average by the spot supplier.
  • Forecast horizon: number of periods for which the forecast is available (1 = current period).
  • Frozen horizon: number of periods in the forecast horizon for which the forecast is frozen (i.e. does not change over time; 1 = current period frozen).
